Easy Spaghetti Recipe

Description

If you're like me, you're someone who likes to eat healthy and tasty meals, but you don't always have time to cook. Sometimes, you want to break away from your typical protein, rice, and veggie meal, but you don't want to spend more than your usual time cooking.

Enter: the Easy Spaghetti Recipe. This meal is ready in just under an hour, and although there's nothing quite like Mom's spaghetti, this recipe comes darn close.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 12 ounces spaghetti
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2/3 cup water

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a medium skillet. Add chopped onions, and cook until tender. Add ground beef, and brown. Stir in salt and turmeric. Dilute tomato paste in water, and add to the beef mixture. Cook for 30 minutes over medium heat.
  2. Cook spaghetti in a large kettle of boiling salted water according to package directions. Drain well.
  3. Combine beef mixture and noodles in a large kettle. Cover, and cook over medium heat for 30 to 40 minutes.
